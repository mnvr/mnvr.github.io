Free lunches

Some people rake millions out of thin air, even though the books tell me the markets are efficient. I see 10xers in front of my eyes, while others deny their existence (while celebrating olympic medals of exceptional achievement).

There are free lunches. The margins might be thin, but arbitrage works; even the future isn't evenly distributed.

There are free lunches. If there weren't, evolution wouldn't work.

Manav RathiMarch 2025
